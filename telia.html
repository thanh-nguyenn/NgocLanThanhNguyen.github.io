12. FAQs about Telia in Finland

Q: Does Telia offer 5G service in Finland?

Yes, Telia has rolled out 5G coverage in all major cities like Helsinki, Tampere, Oulu etc. So 5G-compatible devices enjoy incredibly fast speeds on suitable Telia plans.

Q: Can I use Telia SIM in other Nordic countries?

Telia allows affordable roaming across Denmark, Sweden, Norway for calling, texting and moderate data usage. Some packs even have a daily EU roaming bundle.

Q: How to check data used on Telia SIM?

Dial *101*11*4# to check mobile internet usage statistics like data consumed, days left etc. on your Telia prepaid connection.

Q: Does Telia SIM work in Russia?

While in Finland, Telia has good coverage extending to border towns near Russia. But once you cross over into Russia, there may be connectivity issues or roaming charges. Consider buying a local Russia SIM instead for that leg of your travel.
