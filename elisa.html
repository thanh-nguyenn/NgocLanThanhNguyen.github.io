11. FAQs about Elisa in Finland

Does Elisa offer unlimited data plans?

No, Elisa doesn’t currently offer fully unlimited data on prepaid or tourist plans. But many monthly plans have unlimited data. As a tourist, you can buy multiple data packs up to 25 GB to meet your usage needs.

Can I use Elisa SIM in other European countries?

Elisa has fair usage policies on roaming. You can use up to 9 GB in 12 month period while traveling in the EU without extra charges. Beyond the limit, a small surcharge applies.

How to check data usage on my Elisa SIM?

You can check mobile data usage by dialing *100# or sending TILI to 18258. This displays data consumed from plan. Monitor closely to avoid overuse charges.

Can I buy Elisa SIM online before reaching Finland?

Elisa Finland doesn’t directly sell SIM cards online to ship internationally. You can only buy in Finland or ask someone locally to purchase for you. Easiest option is getting SIM card at airport or downtown stores.
